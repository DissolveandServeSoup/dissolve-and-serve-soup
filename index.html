We've been Making This Delicious Soup since 2024

We are 3 friends who one day had a wonderful idea about how to have a meal prepared and served in 5 minutes or less. We thought that soup would be the best idea since it is the least sold for quick cooking.

This product consists of everything that is in a soup, for example carrot, pumpkin, onion, chicken, a little chili for flavor, pepper, salt, etc., all wrapped in a ball of rice paper. Tell your family and friends about it. Thank you.
